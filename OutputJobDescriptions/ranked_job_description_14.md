### Job Description
Sunrock Distributed Generation is a developer, long-term operator, and financier of solar assets deployed on the properties of businesses, municipalities and nonprofits across the USA. Leveraging an unmatched network of trusted partners and installers, tech-enabled operational efficiency, and a proprietary underwriting method, we design solar and distributed generation solutions tailored to the needs of organizations previously unable to access the benefits of solar energy. Our approach emphasizes enabling businesses to maximize the benefits of solar and distributed energy solutions, regardless of project size. We offer Power Purchase Agreements (PPA) with zero upfront investment, positioning us as the trusted platform for commercial solar DG projects of any size.  We have received investment from Greenbacker Development Opportunities Fund I, L.P. (“GDEV”), a leading infrastructure fund that has deployed approximately $3.8 Bn in capital in sustainable infrastructure investments, acquiring, constructing and operating over 3.3GW renewable energy assets.  We are looking for a Solar Project Manager to join our top-notch team of experts building solar, storage, electric vehicle, and other distributed energy solutions across the U.S. We are seeking an individual to lead commercial solar projects through engineering, utility, city permit and installation milestones, including supervising, coordinating, and communicating with vendors and subcontractors. An ideal candidate will have prior experience constructing energy projects, knowledge in electrical / structural engineering, a technology mindset and an interest in long term relationship building. Take advantage of the opportunity to have a big impact on the growth of clean and renewable technologies!
