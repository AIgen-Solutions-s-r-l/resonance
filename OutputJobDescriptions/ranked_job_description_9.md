### Job Description
We are seeking a visionary Chief Data Officer (CDO) to lead our customers data strategy, governance, and analytics initiatives. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in data management and analytics, with a strong emphasis on leveraging modern data platforms, particularly Snowflake. The CDO will play a pivotal role in transforming data into actionable insights that drive business growth and innovation. Key Responsibilities Data Strategy Development: Design and implement a comprehensive data strategy that aligns with the company’s business objectives and leverages the capabilities of Snowflake Data Warehouse.Data Governance and Compliance: Establish and enforce data governance policies to ensure data quality, security, and compliance with relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).Data Architecture and Management: Oversee data architecture design and management, ensuring efficient data storage, retrieval, and processing using Snowflake’s features.Analytics and Insights: Lead efforts to develop advanced analytics and business intelligence solutions, transforming raw data into actionable insights for various business units.Team Leadership: Build and manage a high-performing data team, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous improvement.Stakeholder Engagement: Collaborate with executive leadership and key stakeholders to promote data-driven decision-making across the organization.Budget Management: Manage the budget for data initiatives, ensuring optimal allocation of resources for data tools, technologies, and personnel.Emerging Technologies: Stay abreast of industry trends and emerging technologies in data management and analytics, making recommendations for adoption as appropriate. Qualifications Experience: 10+ years of experience in data management, analytics, or related fields, with at least 5 years in a senior leadership role.Technical Expertise: Proven experience with Snowflake Data Warehouse and other cloud-based data solutions; familiarity with ETL tools, data modeling, and data visualization tools.Leadership Skills: Strong leadership and team management skills, with a track record of building and leading successful data teams.Analytical Mindset: Exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to translate complex data into strategic insights.Communication Skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to present complex information clearly to various audiences. Benefits Competitive salary and performance-based bonusesComprehensive health benefitsRetirement plan with company matchingOpportunities for professional development and continuous learningFlexible work environment
