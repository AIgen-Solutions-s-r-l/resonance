### Job Description
Job Title: Retail Operations Specialist

Location: Manitoba, Canada

Salary Range: $50,000 - $60,000

Notice Period: 2 months

Industry: Retail

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and results-driven Retail Operations Specialist to join our team in Manitoba, Canada. The ideal candidate will play a crucial role in enhancing our retail operations, focusing on loss prevention, visual merchandising, and effective inventory management.

Key Responsibilities:
- Implement loss prevention strategies to minimize shrinkage and ensure the security of merchandise.
- Develop and execute visually appealing merchandising displays that attract customers and drive sales.
- Utilize inventory software to maintain accurate stock levels, perform regular audits, and manage reordering schedules.
- Collaborate with the marketing team to align visual merchandising with promotional campaigns.
- Analyze sales data and customer feedback using CRM software to enhance the shopping experience and improve customer retention.
- Prepare reports and presentations using Microsoft Office tools to communicate findings and recommendations to management.
- Adapt to changing retail environments and customer preferences, demonstrating flexibility in approach and strategy.

Qualifications:
- Proven experience in retail operations or a similar role, with a strong understanding of loss prevention techniques.
- Proficiency in visual merchandising and the ability to create compelling displays that drive customer engagement.
- Experience with inventory software and a solid understanding of inventory management practices.
- Strong skills in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) for reporting and analysis purposes.
- Knowledge of CRM software and its application in enhancing customer relationships and loyalty.
- Exceptional adaptability and problem-solving skills in a fast-paced retail environment.

Skills Required:
- Loss Prevention
- Visual Merchandising
- Inventory Software
- Microsoft Office
- CRM Software
- Adaptability

If you are passionate about retail operations and have the skills and experience we are looking for, we encourage you to apply and become a vital part of our team!
